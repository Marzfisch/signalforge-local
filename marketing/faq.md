# FAQ

## Does SignalForge Local guarantee rankings?

No. The service identifies and implements practical improvements, but rankings depend on many factors outside the operator's control.

## Does the audit use private data?

The audit uses information entered by the user. The default version stores lead data in the browser. Optional Cloudflare deployment can store submitted records in D1.

## Who is the best fit?

Owner-operated local businesses with existing customers, a public profile, and a need for clearer online acquisition.

## Who is not a fit?

Businesses needing paid ads management, complex enterprise SEO, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or guaranteed performance claims.

## Why productized packages?

Fixed scopes keep the business affordable, repeatable, and easier to automate.
