Most local business websites do not fail because they are ugly.

They fail because a mobile visitor cannot answer three questions quickly:

1. Are you near me?
2. Do you solve my exact problem?
3. What should I do next?

## Short Post

Local SEO gets abstract fast. A useful first pass is simple:

- Is the profile complete?
- Are reviews current?
- Does each service have a local page?
- Can mobile visitors call or book immediately?
- Are review requests automated?

That is the checklist behind SignalForge Local.
