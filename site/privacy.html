SignalForge Local

Privacy Policy

Effective July 4, 2026.

SignalForge Local collects business information submitted through the audit form, including business name, location, website, review metrics, and contact email when provided. In the default static version, this information is stored only in the visitor's browser local storage. If the optional Cloudflare backend is enabled, submitted audit data is stored in the configured Cloudflare D1 database for follow-up.

We use submitted information to generate audit results, prioritize recommendations, and respond to requested follow-up. We do not sell personal information. We do not use paid tracking pixels in the zero-cost version.

Visitors can clear local audit records from the admin dashboard or by clearing browser site data. Backend deletion requests should be sent to the business operator through the contact address published on the deployed site.

This policy should be reviewed by a qualified professional before use in regulated industries or jurisdictions with specific privacy obligations.
