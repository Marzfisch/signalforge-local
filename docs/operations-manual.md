# Operations Manual

## Daily

- Check new audit leads.
- Reply to qualified leads within one business day.
- Send up to 10 personalized prospecting emails.
- Log every reply and objection.

## Weekly

- Generate the growth report.
- Publish one educational post.
- Improve one recommendation rule based on lead patterns.
- Ask active customers for missing inputs.
- Archive completed work samples.

## Fulfillment Workflow

1. Review the audit score and weakest category.
2. Confirm the business's highest-value service and location.
3. Select the smallest paid offer that solves the immediate bottleneck.
4. Deliver only the scoped assets.
5. Send before-and-after summary.
6. Ask for testimonial, referral, and permission to create an anonymized case study.

## Quality Standard

Every paid delivery must be specific enough that the owner can implement it or approve it without another strategy meeting. Avoid vague instructions such as "improve SEO" or "post more content."

## Refund Policy Recommendation

For early trust, offer a simple satisfaction guarantee on one-time packages: if the customer says the delivered recommendations are not specific or useful, revise once or refund. Do not guarantee rankings, traffic, or revenue.
