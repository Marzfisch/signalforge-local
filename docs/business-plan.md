# Business Plan

## Business

SignalForge Local sells productized local growth implementation for small businesses. The free audit creates value before the sale and identifies which owners have urgent visibility, reputation, conversion, or automation gaps.

## Customer

Primary customer:

- Owner-operated local service businesses with 1-3 locations
- Categories: dental, med spa, HVAC, plumbing, roofing, therapy, legal, fitness, specialty retail
- Existing revenue but inconsistent online acquisition
- Has a website or Google Business Profile but limited marketing operations

## Pain

Small business owners are asked to manage marketing, reviews, websites, social content, analytics, and customer follow-up without specialist support. They want practical fixes, not dashboards or vague strategy.

## Offer Ladder

| Offer | Price | Delivery | Margin Logic |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Free Audit | $0 | Instant browser report | Lead generation |
| Starter Fix | $149 | 24-48 hours | Fast manual implementation from templates |
| Growth Sprint | $399 | 10 business days | Higher-value implementation package |
| Care Plan | $199/month | Monthly | Recurring monitoring and content operations |

## Fulfillment

Starter Fix deliverables:

- Google Business Profile optimization checklist
- Homepage CTA rewrite
- Review request email and SMS script
- One-page before-and-after report

Growth Sprint deliverables:

- Local service page content brief
- FAQ content and schema JSON-LD
- Review workflow and response templates
- Conversion improvement list
- Measurement plan

Care Plan deliverables:

- Monthly audit refresh
- Four local content briefs
- Review response drafts
- Opportunity report
- Next-month priority list

## Acquisition

Zero-cost channels:

- Direct outreach to businesses with obvious Google profile or website gaps
- Helpful posts in local business groups where self-promotion is allowed
- SEO articles targeting local marketing pain points
- Public case studies after each completed project
- Referral ask after every fulfilled order

## Validation Targets

First 30 days:

- 100 manually reviewed prospects
- 40 personalized audit links sent
- 10 replies
- 3 paid Starter Fix customers
- 1 Growth Sprint conversion

Continue only if at least one paid customer closes. If replies are positive but conversion is weak, lower friction by offering a $49 diagnostic call creditable toward implementation.

## Risks

- Search ranking promises can create legal and trust risk. Mitigation: never guarantee rankings or revenue.
- Manual fulfillment can expand. Mitigation: maintain fixed scopes and repeatable templates.
- Cold outreach can become spammy. Mitigation: personalize, target public business problems, and honor opt-outs.
- Legal pages are not legal advice. Mitigation: user reviews terms before public launch.

## Defensibility

Defensibility comes from compounding local data, repeatable workflows, public proof, category-specific templates, and faster delivery. The first niche should become a vertical playbook before expanding.
