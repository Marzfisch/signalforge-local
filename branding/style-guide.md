# Brand Style Guide

## Name

SignalForge Local

## Positioning

Practical local growth audits and implementation for owner-operated small businesses.

## Voice

- Clear
- Specific
- Calm
- Operational
- No hype
- No ranking guarantees

## Taglines

- Find the visibility leaks costing local customers.
- Practical local growth fixes, scored in minutes.
- Local SEO and automation help without consultant fog.

## Color System

- Ink: `#172026`
- Paper: `#f8faf8`
- Green: `#1c7c54`
- Teal: `#0b7189`
- Coral: `#c8553d`
- Gold: `#c69214`

## Logo Concept

Use a compact square mark with `SF` in strong sans-serif lettering. The symbol should feel like a practical workshop stamp rather than a futuristic AI brand.

Image generation prompt:

```text
Minimal vector-style brand mark for "SignalForge Local", square black stamp with the letters SF, subtle local map grid and signal path motif, clean professional small business consulting identity, flat colors, white background, no gradients, no mockup.
```

## UI Rules

- Cards use 8px radius.
- Use white surfaces on a light paper background.
- Use strong contrast for calls to action.
- Keep language practical and proof-oriented.
